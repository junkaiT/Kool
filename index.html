How often should I service my aircon? ›
Every 3 months for regular use. If you run your aircon daily or have elderly residents or young children at home, quarterly keeps your unit optimal and the air clean.
Why is my aircon still not cold after servicing? ›
If our ΔT reading shows the unit is still underperforming, we'll recommend a chemical wash or KoolJet — based on data, not guesswork. No pressure to upgrade.
What is Delta T and why does it matter? ›
ΔT is the temperature difference between the warm air going into your aircon and the cold air coming out. A healthy Singapore split unit should show 8–11°C. We measure before and after every service so you see the exact improvement.
Do I need to be home during the service? ›
Someone should be home to let our technician in. You don't need to supervise the whole job — most customers go about their day. We'll send the WhatsApp report once it's done.
What if I have an issue after my service? ›
WhatsApp us within 48 hours and we'll return at no charge, no questions asked. That's our service guarantee.
