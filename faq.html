General

How often should I service my aircon? ›
Every 3 months for regular use. If you run your aircon more than 8 hours a day, or have elderly residents or young children, quarterly servicing maintains both performance and air quality.
What is Delta T (ΔT) and why does it matter? ›
ΔT is the temperature difference between the warm air entering your aircon and the cold air coming out. A healthy Singapore split unit should show 8–11°C. We measure it before and after every service so you can see the exact improvement.
Why is my aircon not cold even after servicing? ›
Most commonly: a dirty condenser (outdoor unit), low refrigerant, or a coil that needs a chemical wash. Our ΔT reading will tell us which. We'll recommend the right fix — not the most expensive one.
Do I need to be home during the service? ›
Someone should be home to let our technician in. You don't need to supervise — most customers go about their day. We'll send the WhatsApp report when done.
What if I have an issue after my service? ›
WhatsApp us within 48 hours and we'll return at no charge, no questions asked.
